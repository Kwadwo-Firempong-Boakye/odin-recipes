Hot-Honey-Wings Recipe



Crispy baked chicken wings offer the crunch of fried chicken without deep frying


You can also make these wings crisp in your air fryer too!


If you enjoy mild buffalo wings but want to switch things up a bit, then these offer a unique alternative


Ingredients


  • Butter
  • Hot Sauce
  • Honey
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • 20 chicken wings parts
  • Baking Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Dried Parsley
  • Ginger Powder

Steps


  1. Pat the chicken wings dry, mix the spices and toss the chicken to coat.

  2. Place wings on a parchment-paper lined baking sheet or a baking sheet with a cooling rack. Cook the wings in the oven for 20-25 minutes per side.

  3. Add all ingredients for the sauce to a small bowl. Whisk to incorporate.

  4. Add the wings to a bowl with some of the sauce and toss to coat.

  5. Adjust the bake time for added crispiness. The longer you cook your wings in the oven, the crispier they will become. For saucy wings, I tend to keep my chicken in for closer to 25 minutes per side so that they can still be slightly crisp after I add the hot honey Old Bay sauce.
